General

"jp_pEng" is a DERPG Web App written by テイラー利恵(りえ) (me) out of boredom in aid for Japanese tutoring. The concept is very simple: Click a module, a section, and then finally a lesson and practise! You are also given a chart which you can use to study from, as well as randomly generated Worksheets. There is also a 辞書 for looking up words from the Vocabulary Section. Take full advantage of this Web App that I wrote. Happy practicing! :)

～テイラー利恵
